01.About Me

🌌 Astrophysicist | M.Sc. Physics @ Ariel University (Israel) & NIT Hamirpur (India)

From a small village in Bihar to international research in Israel — my journey is rooted in curiosity about black holes, AGN, and the high-energy universe. I investigate how supermassive black holes shape galaxies and drive cosmic evolution.
